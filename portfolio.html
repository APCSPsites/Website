Sushi Factory Game


A tutorial game that instructs the player how to make two sushi rolls, a California Roll and a Salmon Roll. The player gets to assemble, roll, cut, and plate the sushi.

Fruit Slicer Game


A take on the classic Fruit Ninja game where fruit are thrown up in the air and when clicked they are cut and fall. Unlike the origanal, different fruits are worth different amounts depending on size.

Trench Run


A remix Scratch plane game inspired by the trench run in Star Wars.
